About Game

Small back ground

May 2009 saw the founding of Minecraft. Markus "Notch" Persson used the Java programming language to construct the game. Prior to its official release in November 2011, it was made available for download as a public alpha for personal computers in 2009, with Jens Bergensten taking over development. Since then, it has been ported to a number of platforms, and as of June 2019, it had sold over 180 million copies across all platforms and had over 112 million monthly active players, making it the best-selling video game of all time. A player base of contributors supports the game by making and uploading original maps, minigames, skins, and mods. In addition, the game has been covered in a number of novels and other media.

What makes the game enjoyable

Simply exploring, making things, and building things in this environment is a wonderful and captivating experience. The construction capabilities in Minecraft have been pushed to their extremes by players, who have created magnificent constructions including scale replicas of the USS Enterprise, the Reichstag, and Hogwarts.
